Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

You get 3 complete project analyses with basic features. No credit card required to start.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What file formats do you support for export?

We support PDF reports, DXF and DWG files for CAD import, and CSV data exports.

Which building codes are supported?

We support NBC, Alberta, Ontario, and BC building codes, with more jurisdictions coming soon.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees. You only pay the monthly subscription or per-project fee.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans.
